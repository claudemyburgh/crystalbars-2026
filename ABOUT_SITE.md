# Crystal Bars – Website Analysis
https://www.crystalbars.co.za/

## Business Overview
**Crystal Bars** is a home and property security company based in **Cape Town, South Africa**.  
The company specializes in installing **transparent burglar bars** made from **polycarbonate**, designed to provide security without blocking the view from windows.

Their solutions are primarily aimed at **residential homes**, but may also apply to **small businesses and offices**.

The key selling point of their products is **clear security bars** that maintain the appearance of modern homes while still offering strong protection against intruders.

---

# Products

## 1. Transparent Polycarbonate Burglar Bars
This is the **primary product** offered by the company.

### Features
- Made from **A-grade polycarbonate imported from Europe**
- **6 mm thick** and **35 mm wide bars**
- Tensile breaking strength of approximately **3000 kg**
- **UV protected** to prevent fading or discoloration
- **Rust-free material**
- **Transparent design** to maintain outside visibility

### Benefits
- Maintains the **aesthetic appearance** of windows
- Provides **strong security similar to traditional burglar bars**
- **No maintenance required**
- Does not block **natural light or views**

### Window Compatibility
The burglar bars can be installed on:
- Aluminium windows
- Wooden windows
- Steel windows
- PVC windows

---

## 2. Aluminium Trellis Security Gates
Crystal Bars also offers **expandable aluminium security gates** for doorways and entrances.
